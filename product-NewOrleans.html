Description

Introducing "New Orleans," a captivating bouquet of beautiful fresh flowers that is one of our most sought-after creations. This exquisite arrangement features an ivory large-headed rose, purple freesia, purple lisianthus, and white lilies, accented with eucalyptus and pistache foliage. Perfect for gifting on Easter, Christmas, Mother's Day, birthdays, anniversaries, or any special occasion, "New Orleans" is a stunning expression of love and appreciation.

Hand-selected by our team of talented florists, each bloom is chosen with care and arranged to perfection to showcase the natural beauty of this fresh-cut flower bouquet. The combination of purple and white flowers, including the elegant ivory rose and fragrant purple freesia, is complemented by lush greenery to offset the hues of the petals.

Carefully arranged to create a visually striking bouquet, "New Orleans" is an ideal gift for expressing your heartfelt sentiments. Whether it's to celebrate a milestone, convey gratitude, or simply brighten someone's day, this beautiful bouquet is sure to leave a lasting impression.

Conveniently delivered straight to your door or directly to your chosen recipient, "New Orleans" is a thoughtful and elegant gift that is sure to bring joy and delight to any occasion.
