Description

Introducing our exquisite Box of Pinks, a delightful expression of affection and admiration for your loved ones. This charming ensemble features a captivating assortment of roses in an array of pretty pastel shades, thoughtfully complemented by the addition of purple limonium. Each element is carefully selected to create a harmonious and visually stunning bouquet, sure to brighten anyone's day.

Inside the box, you'll discover a total of twenty roses, meticulously arranged to showcase their natural beauty. Among them are five enchanting lilac roses, symbolizing admiration and enchantment, alongside five pristine white roses, representing purity and sincerity. Adding a touch of romance are five delicate pink roses, evoking feelings of love and gratitude, while five dark pink roses infuse the bouquet with a sense of passion and desire. To complete the ensemble, two stems of purple limonium are included, adding a whimsical touch of color and texture.

With a bouquet length of approximately 40cm, this charming arrangement is both elegant and versatile, making it the perfect gift for a variety of occasions. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or just a spontaneous gesture of love, our Box of Pinks is sure to make a lasting impression. So go ahead, show someone special how much you care with this beautiful bouquet that speaks volumes of your affection.
