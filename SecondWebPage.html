Who We Are

Welcome to House of Petals, the place to turn to in the UK for gorgeous flower arrangements. Our commitment to sourcing only the freshest and properly farmed flowers stems from our love for spreading smiles on peoples faces. Each arrangement is carefully handmade by our skilled florists, who make sure that each arrangement is an unique work of beauty.

In addition to flowers, we work with local artists to provide a thoughtfully selected selection of complementary gifts, giving your gesture an added personal touch.

House of Petals is committed to assisting you in remembering life's important events. We offer reliable delivery services that include both calm countryside and busy cities.
