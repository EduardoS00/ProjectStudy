Description

Indulge in the charm of rustic luxury with our vintage-inspired floral arrangement, meticulously curated to bring timeless elegance to your space. Bursting with an exquisite array of natural and lightly dyed flowers, including delicate wildflowers, creamy and pink blooms, this bouquet evokes a sense of tranquility and natural beauty. Lush grasses, along with accents of wheat and oat, add depth and texture to the floral display, enhancing its rustic allure.

Each stem is carefully selected and arranged by our skilled team at House of Petals, ensuring a harmonious blend of colors and shapes. Meticulously trimmed and tied with twine, every detail is thoughtfully considered to create a stunning visual impact. The arrangement is then lovingly wrapped in brown kraft paper, adding a touch of understated elegance to its presentation.

Arriving pre-arranged and ready to display, our bouquet is a hassle-free way to add a touch of sophistication to your home decor. Whether adorning your living room coffee table or gracing your bedside nightstand, this floral masterpiece is sure to captivate and delight. Packaged in a sturdy cardboard box, it's also an ideal gift for loved ones or a delightful treat for yourself.

Sustainable and long-lasting, these dried flowers require minimal maintenance, making them a practical and eco-friendly choice for any home. With proper care and gentle handling, they will continue to bring joy and beauty to your surroundings for years to come, serving as a timeless reminder of the simple pleasures found in nature's bounty.
