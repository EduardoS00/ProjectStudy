Description

Introducing our enchanting bouquet, "Fall Combination," a stunning arrangement that captures the essence of the autumn season. This exquisite bouquet features a captivating blend of roses, carnations, and tulips, complemented by lush fillers and foliage. Each bloom is carefully selected to evoke the warm and rich hues of fall, creating a harmonious tapestry of colors and textures.

With dimensions measuring 26cm in width and 32cm in height, this bouquet is the perfect size to adorn any space with its beauty. Whether placed as a centerpiece on your dining table or presented as a heartfelt gift for a special occasion, "Fall Combination" is sure to make a lasting impression.

Please note that as all fresh flowers, fillers, and foliage are seasonal, they are subject to changes based on availability. However, rest assured that our skilled florists will ensure that each bouquet maintains its enchanting allure and captures the essence of the fall season.

Suitable for a variety of occasions including anniversaries, birthdays, congratulations, and romantic gestures such as proposals, "Fall Combination" is a versatile choice that speaks volumes of love, warmth, and appreciation. Choose from small, medium, or large sizes to suit your preference and make any occasion extra special with this exquisite bouquet.
